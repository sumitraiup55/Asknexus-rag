ASKNEXUS CUSTOMER FAQ DOCUMENT
Title: Customer FAQ
Department: support
Access Roles: admin, customer

This document contains customer support and frequently asked questions for AskNexus customers.

1. Support Hours
AskNexus customer support is available from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.
Customers can raise support tickets from the customer dashboard.

2. Refund Policy
Customers can request a refund within 7 business days of purchase if the service has not been heavily used.
Refund requests are reviewed by the support team before approval.

3. Ticket Response Time
Normal support tickets are answered within 24 hours.
Urgent account access issues are prioritized and usually reviewed within 6 hours.

4. Plan Upgrade
Customers can upgrade their plan from the billing section of the dashboard.
After payment confirmation, upgraded features are activated immediately.

5. Account Help
If a customer cannot log in, they should use the Forgot Password option or contact support@asknexus.com.

6. Data Privacy
Customer uploaded documents are only used to answer questions inside their organization workspace.
AskNexus does not share customer documents with other organizations.
